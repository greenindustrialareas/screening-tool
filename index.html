Green Industrial Areas
Screening Tool

Screening tool for industrial symbiosis readiness

A self-assessment for businesses exploring industrial symbiosis and resource collaboration. Takes 10–15 minutes, no account required. You'll receive a downloadable PDF report.

About
Part of the GreenIndustrialAreas Interreg Baltic Sea Region project, developed by Kalundborg Symbiosis.
What to Prepare
Have a general sense of your energy, water and material flows and your organisation's green priorities.
Privacy & Data Protection
GDPR compliant. This tool runs entirely in your browser — no answers, scores, or personal data are ever transmitted to any server or third party. Your responses are saved only to your browser's localStorage so you can return to the questionnaire; this data never leaves your device. Hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs as per their privacy statement, but no additional tracking or analytics are applied by this tool.
